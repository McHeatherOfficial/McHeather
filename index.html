About McHeather

McHeather is a genre-defying artist and sonic architect from South Africa, fusing everything from hip-hop and R&B to pop, house, amapiano, gospel, reggae, and afrobeats — all grounded in rich, soulful chord progressions.

As an artist, producer, and full-spectrum engineer (recording, mixing, and mastering), McHeather doesn't just make music — he builds it from the ground up. His productions are layered with emotion, creativity, and attention to detail, making each track a unique sonic experience.

Beyond music, McHeather shares his expertise with artists, providing guidance and professional studio services to help them craft their signature sound.
